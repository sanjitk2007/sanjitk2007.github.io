About Me

Work

I'm currently a software engineering intern at Humana, working on their CenterWell Pharmacy and Military Technology team, building an AI-driven workforce routing optimization platform for field technicians across the nation. I'm studying Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in the School of Computer Science. I've previously worked with or done research at:

  • CMU Human-Computer Interaction Institute (2023 – present): AI research on tutor training systems for 3 years.
  • Stanford Center for AI in Medicine and Imaging (2024): Building respiratory disease classification pipelines.
  • Harvard READS Lab (2024 – 2025): Educational ML and psychometrics.

Interests

I'm interested in large-language models, agentic AI, voice agents, and human-computer interaction. Lately, I've been building simulation tools for software and hardware agents integrating LLMs, web and mobile apps for CMU's premier software organization, and machine learning projects on complex datasets. Outside of that, I enjoy soccer, spikeball, and photography.

Research Accomplishments

My work focuses on leveraging AI to optimize education through advancing tutor training analysis and comparing frontier models on different nuanced, humanistic tasks in academic settings. I've presented at numerous international conferences and been cited by many of the top researchers in the field.

  • 2nd Best Poster: IEEE MIT Undergraduate Research Technology Conference.
  • Best Paper: IEEE International Workshop on Artificial Intelligence and Education (Tokyo, Japan).
  • Presenter: 26th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education (Palermo, Italy): Top conference globally for AI in education.
  • Presenter: 33rd International Congress of Psychology (Prague, Czech Republic).
  • Youngest Presenter: AAAI Workshop on AI for Education (Vancouver, Canada) & AAAI Workshop on Innovation and Responsibility in AI-Supported Education (Philadelphia, USA).
  • Presenter: 15th ACM Learning Analytics and Knowledge Conference (Dublin, Ireland).
